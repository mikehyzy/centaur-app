How it works

Three stages.
One verdict.

Stage 01

Your take
first

Before you see AI's response, you write your reasoning. One field. No structure. What do you actually think?

A timer runs invisibly. The time you spend framing is one of the signals Centaur captures.

Stage 02

Compare
the output

Paste the AI's response. Your framing stays visible above — you can see the delta between what you thought and what AI produced.

Select any part of the output to mark friction or challenge.

Stage 03

The
verdict

Centaur classifies the session. One word. No score breakdown. An instrument reading settling into place.

Add a note if you want one. Save. Move on.
